HOW TO RECORD
================================================================================

## With Phone:
- Good lighting (window light)
- Stabilize phone (tripod or stack of books)
- Record landscape (YouTube) + portrait (TikTok)
- Use voiceover for tech details

## Free Editing:
- CapCut (phone) - easiest
- DaVinci Resolve (desktop) - more power
- OpenShot (desktop) - simple

## Screen Recording:
- Use this laptop's screen!
- Record me "thinking" and responding
